    Do's

    • Rinse containers before recycling.
    • Flatten cardboard to save space.
    • Separate wet (organic) and dry (recyclable) waste.

    Don'ts

    • Don't put liquids or food-soaked items in recyclables.
    • Don't mix hazardous chemicals with regular trash.
    • Don't put batteries in general bins.
